Discover Argentina – Website Plan

Site Name

Discover Argentina

This name is clear, inviting, and directly communicates the purpose of the site: to promote tourism in Argentina. It's easy to remember and ideal for branding. No domain purchase is required, but visitargentina.travel or argentinatourism.gob.ar would be suitable.

Site Purpose

  • To showcase Argentina's most beautiful and culturally rich tourist destinations.
  • To provide travelers with inspiration, information, and tools to plan their trip.
  • To collect visitor interest via a newsletter subscription form.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A traveler from Spain wants to know the top natural attractions in Argentina before planning a 2-week trip. Where should they go?
  • Scenario 2: A family in Canada is looking for safe and wonderful destinations in South America. Can Argentina offer that?

Color Schema

Deep Blue (#1e3a8a): Used for headers, navigation, and primary buttons. Represents trust and the vast skies and waters of Argentina.

Golden Yellow (#fbbf24): Accent color for highlights, calls to action, and icons. Inspired by the sun and Argentina's national flag.

Emerald Green (#059669): Used for secondary buttons and nature-related accents. Reflects the lush forests and natural beauty.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif – clean, modern, and elegant.
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif – highly readable on all devices.
  • Code/Technical: Consolas, monospace – only for dev references.

Note: Google Fonts will be used in the final website. For this planning document, system fonts are used for simplicity.
